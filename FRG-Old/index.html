Birational Geometry and Singularities In Zero and Positive Characteristic


This website records activities of an National Science Foundation Focused Research Group, DMS-#1265261/1501115. Check here to find upcoming activities and links to preprints and publications.

The goal of this project is to bring together a group of researchers with experience in a broad array of topics in higher dimensional algebraic geometry, in order to make progress in two closely related areas: birational geometry in positive characteristics and the theory of singularities and linear series arising in the minimal model program.
