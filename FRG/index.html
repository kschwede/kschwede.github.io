FRG: Algebraic Geometry and Singularities in Positive and Mixed Characteristic


News

Check out the Special month at the University of Michigan, May 8th - June 2nd, 2023.

About

This NSF Focus Research Grant award, FRG: Algebraic Geometry and Singularities in Positive and Mixed Characteristic is designed to study singularities in positive and mixed characteristic and to apply what is discovered to key questions in algebraic geometry and related fields.
This is a collaborative project involving the institutions:
  • Purdue University
  • Princeton University
  • The University of Michigan
  • The University of Utah
  • The University of Washington
    • Under NSF grants #1952366, 1951376, 1952399, 1952522, 1952531, and 2139613.
